Access & Launch 9 ITEMS
  • LauncherOpen the Launcher
  • QuickActionsMenuOpen a list with all QuickMenu Actions
  • AppsList of bookmarked apps, grouped by categories, such as Apps, Productivity, Editors

  • DesktopFilesShow Desktop Files, good if you want to keep desktop files hidden to have a clear desktop
  • NotionBrowse and Search through your Notion board
  • MemosSave memos and notes for later
  • WorkspacesLoad Workspaces.

  • AudioManage Output/Input Devices, and per app Volume
  • MediaControlGeneric Media control. Left Click to play, Right for next, middle for previous
  • MusicServerLocal and Subsonic Music Player. It supports media interaction such as double press next, right click for next, middle click for previous and drag up down to seek up/down
  • AppAudioControl1Customizable per-app Audio Control. Left Click for pause, Right Click for Next, Middle Click for previous, hold and drag up down for seek up/down.
  • AppAudioControl2Same as above, you can control the track for a specific app, such as Youtube Music (need custom Chrome Extension for global hotkeys) or MusicBee, Namida, etc.
  • AppAudioControl3 -
  • AppAudioControl4 -
  • AppAudioControl5 -
  • MicMuteOne-tap microphone mute/unmute.
Visual & Capture 5 ITEMS
  • ScreenDrawOpens Screen Draw. Check Hotkeys to see how to enable it.
  • FancyShotOpens Screen Capture Live or Freeze
  • ColorPickerOpens Color Picker
  • SpotlightOpens Spotlight, check Hotkeys to see how to control it.
  • FancyShotScreen Capture with stylized capture. Slighly outdated for ScreenCapture
Security & Privacy 6 ITEMS
  • VaultSave your private API keys and secrets. It uses one way ecryption (PBE with AES-CBC) so do not lose your password! You can also not specify a password.
  • AuthenticatorIt Generated One-Time Password. You can add them by backup file or QR Scan. You can also encrypt the file (PBE with AES-CBC).
  • ClipboardHistoryBrowse through clipboard history, can manage how many days you retain it.
  • ClearKeyboardBlocks keyboard input if you want to clean your keyboard, or to make your PC cat resistent when you are idle.
  • PinWindowMake latest active window Always on Top
  • CloseOnFocusLossIt toggles QuickMenu action that it closes on blur/focus loss
System & Environment 9 ITEMS
  • TaskManagerOpens Task Manager
  • VirtualDesktopRight click for next desktop, Left for previous. It cycles.
  • ToggleTaskbarShows or hides the Windows taskbar.
  • ToggleWallpaperModeSwitched between black screen or wallapapers
  • ToggleDesktopIt hides all the opened apps and focuses on desktop
  • ToggleWindowsThemeToggles between light and dark windows Theme. Can be scheduled from settings.
  • HideDesktopFilesIt hides/shows desktop files, if you want to keep the desktop clean
  • ToggleHiddenFilesToggles hidden-file visibility in Explorer.
  • AlwaysAwakeKeeps the machine awake during focused work.
